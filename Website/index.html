CSCI-716 Project

Robot Motion Planning

Implementation of advanced computational geometry algorithms to navigate point robots efficiently and safely through complex environments. Motion planning is a critical component of robotics, enabling robots to autonomously traverse from one location to another while avoiding obstacles and collisions.
